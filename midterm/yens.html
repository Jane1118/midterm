    inference:
    Rice noodles and soup: Dong 59,000-69,000 (USD 2.65-3.1)
    Main course(clay pot, hotpot, something with meat and rice): Dong About 100,000(USD 4.5)
    drinks: Dong 20,000-40,000 (USD 0.9-1.8)

    Good to know

    • DO!

      HOTPOT 100% needs to try, especially the fresh squid.
      The flavor Vietnams white wine is impressive and very hot, worth trying.

    • better not

      This restaurant is always very busy in an rush hour. I think a proper time for lunch might be after 2:00 pm, and for dinner might be before 6:00pm.
      I don't think the juice here is as good as its hotpot cause there are many super good small cafes and smoothies shops to exlpore.

    • tips

      Since many russians live here, the menu is written by Vietnamese, English and Russian. Waiters here can speak fluent English, Russian and even Spanish!! Some of them are very handsome!!
      They accept both USD and Vitenam Dong, but USD might be a little bit more expensive cause restaurants in Vietnam tend to use a higher currency exchange rate.
